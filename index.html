Leonard Nimoy

as Spock

1931 - 2015

His most well known role was that of Spock in the Star Trek franchise. The character is a half-Vulcan Starfleet officer. Together with James Kirk and the crew of the USS Enterprise , they explore the far reaches of space. Leonard portrayed Spock from 1964, in Star Trek: The Original Series to 2013 in the recent reboot film, Star Trek Into Darkness .

Timeline

  • 1931 Born in Boston, MA.
  • 1951 First acting role in Queen for a Day.
  • 1964 Filmed the first pilot episode for Star Trek.
  • 1966 Starred in Star Trek: The Original Series until 1969.
  • 1979 Starred in the first of 8 Star Trek films.
  • 2015 Passed away in Los Angeles, CA.
